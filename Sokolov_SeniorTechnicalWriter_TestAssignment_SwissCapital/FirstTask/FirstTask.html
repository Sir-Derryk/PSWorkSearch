Transfer Money to Another Account

To transfer money to someone else's bank account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your online banking platform and sign in with your credentials.
  2. Select Money in/out from the main menu.
  3. Select the Transfer tab.
  4. Click To someone else's account. A panel opens where you can specify the recipient's details.
  5. Specify the recipient's ID and click Continue.
  6. Complete the recipient and bank information:
    • Select EUR or USD.
    • Specify the recipient's full name.
    • Select the recipient's bank country.
    • Specify the city where the bank is located.
    • Select the bank's SWIFT code.
    • Specify the recipient's complete address.
  7. If your transfer requires an intermediary bank, complete these fields:
    • Select the country.
    • Select the bank code.
  8. Select Next.
  9. Complete the transfer details:
    • Select individual or business entity.
    • Upload any required files (if needed).
    • Specify the transfer amount in selected currency.
    • Select standard or guaranteed delivery.
    • Specify the reason for the transfer.
  10. Review your transfer details and click Pay to complete the transaction.
