Transfer Money to Another Account

What you'll do

Send money from your account to someone else's bank account. You can transfer money in EUR or USD to accounts worldwide.

Before you start

Have this information ready:

  • Recipient's full name and address
  • Recipient's bank name and SWIFT code
  • Transfer amount and currency (EUR or USD)
  • Purpose of the transfer
  • Any required documents (for large transfers)

Steps

1 Sign in to online banking

Go to your online banking platform and sign in with your credentials.

2 Go to Money in/out

Select Money in/out from the main menu.

3 Open the Transfer tab

Select the Transfer tab to view transfer options.

4 Start a transfer

Click To someone else's account. A panel opens where you can specify the recipient's details.

5 Specify recipient ID

Specify the recipient's ID and click Continue.

6 Specify recipient and bank details

Complete the required fields:

Recipient Information

  • Currency: Select EUR or USD
  • Recipient name: Specify the recipient's full name
  • Recipient address: Specify the recipient's complete address

Bank Information

  • Bank country: Select the recipient's bank country
  • Bank city: Specify the city where the bank is located
  • SWIFT code: Select the bank's SWIFT code
ℹ️ Optional: If the transfer requires an intermediary bank, complete the intermediary bank fields (country and code).
7 Continue to transfer details

Click Next to proceed to the final step.

8 Complete transfer details

Fill in the remaining information:

Transfer Details

  • Recipient type: Select whether the recipient is an individual or business
  • Amount: Specify the transfer amount in your selected currency
  • Fee type: Select standard or guaranteed delivery
  • Transfer purpose: Specify the reason for the transfer
  • Supporting documents: Upload any required files (if applicable)
9 Complete the transfer

Review your transfer details and click Pay to complete the transaction.

⚠️ Important: Double-check all details before confirming. International transfers cannot be cancelled once processed.

What happens next

After you complete the transfer:

  • You'll receive a confirmation email with the transaction details
  • The money will be debited from your account immediately
  • Transfer time depends on the destination country and fee type selected
  • You can track the transfer status in your online banking

Need help?

If you have questions about your transfer, contact customer support through your online banking platform or call the support hotline.
